INFO RE: PUBLIC

PUBLIC – is a creative platform that produces multimedia content and apparel as tools to raise awareness about critical social justice issues. We collaborate with writers, designers, and creators to build a community that promotes aesthetic excellence, productive dialogue, and communal good. PUBLIC sheds light on policies, facilities, services, and infrastructures that exist openly and relate to the people as a whole.

PUBLIC

(adjective, noun)

1. of, relating to, or concerning the people as a whole; the community.

2. done, perceived, or existing in open view.

PUBLIC

exists for ordinary people.

exists to expose injustice.

Dossier 001 release

India, S377, and Homosexual Identity

In this article, Eleanor Brooks recounts her interview with Dakshnamoarthy Natarajan, a homosexual Indian man, as a means to discuss homosexual identity and gay rights in India. She retells parts of D’s experience in his own words, focusing on his to personal journey to acknowledge his gay identity in a country where homosexuality was both illegal and socially rejected.

NYC subway's mobility – a public irony

In this article, Araya Wongwan deconstructs the New York City's enhantment of mobility through examining the city's integral representation of that idealisation: the subway. The 'public' infrastructure imagined to bridge gaps, from space to race to class, is in fact an exclusive private offered to those with the privilege to afford mobility.
